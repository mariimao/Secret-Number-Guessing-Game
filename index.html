Instructions

Welcome to the Secret Number Guessing Game! Here's how to play:

Objective

You and the computer need to guess each others' secret 3-digit number (no repeating digits, no zeros) in the fewest attempts (max 7 attempts).

How to Play

  • Each player chooses a secret three-digit number.
  • Players take turns guessing each other's number.
  • After each guess, feedback is given:
    • "+" for each correct digit in the correct position.
    • "–" for each correct digit in the wrong position.
  • The winner is the player who guesses the opponent's number in the fewest attempts.

Example

If the secret number is 589 and the guess is 982, the feedback would be "+-" because:

  • 8 is in the correct position.
  • 9 is correct but in the wrong position.
